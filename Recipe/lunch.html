Veg Biriyani

  1. Sauté onions in ghee until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté.
  3. Stir in mixed vegetables and biryani masala, cooking for a few minutes.
  4. Add rinsed basmati rice and water, season with salt.
  5. Cook until rice is tender. Garnish with mint leaves before serving.

Chickpea Curry

  1. Cook soaked chickpeas until soft.
  2. Sauté onions, add ginger-garlic paste and tomatoes, cooking until soft.
  3. Add cooked chickpeas and chole masala, simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Garnish with coriander and serve with rice or naan.

Palak Paneer

  1. Blanch spinach and blend into a paste.
  2. Sauté onions, ginger-garlic, and tomatoes until soft.
  3. Add spinach paste, paneer cubes, and garam masala
  4. Cook for a few minutes, adding cream before serving.

Aloo Gobi

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and sauté onions.
  2. Add ginger, turmeric, potatoes, and cauliflower, mixing well.
  3. Cook with water until vegetables are tender.
  4. Sprinkle coriander powder before serving.

Pulao

  1. Sauté whole spices and onions in ghee.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and sauté for a few minutes.
  3. Add rinsed basmati rice and water, season with salt.
  4. Cook until rice is fluffy and serve hot.

Dal Tadka (Spiced Lentils)

  1. Cook lentils until soft.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee, add cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Stir in tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  4. Add lentils and simmer. Garnish with coriander leaves.

Kadai Paneer

  1. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic in oil.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes and bell peppers.
  3. Stir in paneer cubes and kadai masala, cooking until flavors meld.
  4. Serve hot with roti or naan.

Sambar

  1. Cook toor dal with vegetables until soft.
  2. In a separate pan, heat oil, add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  3. Mix sambar powder and tamarind paste into cooked dal.
  4. Simmer and serve with rice.

Paneer Butter Masala

  1. Heat butter, sauté ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomato puree and cook until thick.
  3. Stir in cream, garam masala, and sugar.
  4. Add paneer and cook for a few minutes. Serve with naan.

Masoor Dal (Red Lentils)

  1. Cook lentils until soft.
  2. Sauté onions and ginger in oil, add tomatoes and turmeric.
  3. Mix in cooked lentils, simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Serve with rice or roti.

Bhindi Masala (Okra Stir-Fry)

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds and sauté onions.
  2. Add chopped okra and sauté until cooked.
  3. Mix in tomatoes and spices, cooking for a few more minutes.
  4. Serve hot with roti.

Dum Aloo (Spiced Baby Potatoes)

  1. Boil and peel baby potatoes.
  2. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic in oil, add yogurt and spices.
  3. Stir in potatoes, coating well, and cook until heated through.
  4. Serve with naan.

Egg Curry

  1. Hard-boil and peel eggs.
  2. Sauté onions and ginger-garlic in oil, add tomatoes and curry powder.
  3. Add eggs to the mixture, simmering for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice.

Lemon Rice

  1. Cook rice and let it cool.
  2. Heat oil, add mustard seeds, peanuts, and curry leaves.
  3. Mix in turmeric and cooked rice, stirring well.
  4. Squeeze lemon juice before serving.

Stuffed Paratha

  1. Mash boiled potatoes, mix in spices.
  2. Roll out dough, stuff with potato mixture, and seal.
  3. Roll out again and cook on a hot griddle with oil.
  4. Serve with yogurt or pickle.
