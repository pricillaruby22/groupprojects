Poha

  1. Rinse poha.
  2. Sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, and onion.
  3. Add turmeric, peanuts, and poha.
  4. Mix, cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Garnish with lemon and coriander.

Idli

  1. Pour batter into greased idli molds.
  2. Steam for 10 minutes.
  3. Cool slightly, remove, and serve with chutney.

Aloo Paratha

  1. Mash potatoes, add spices.
  2. Roll dough, add filling, seal, and roll again.
  3. Cook with oil until golden.

Upma

  1. Roast semolina.
  2. Sauté mustard seeds, curry leaves, and veggies.
  3. Add water, then semolina.
  4. Stir until thick.

Masala Dosa

  1. Prepare potato filling.
  2. Spread dosa batter on tawa, add filling.
  3. Cook until crispy.

Sabudana Khichdi

  1. Sauté cumin, chilies, and potatoes.
  2. Add sabudana and peanuts.
  3. Cook for 5 minutes.

Pesarattu (Moong Dal Dosa)

  1. Grind soaked dal with spices.
  2. Spread batter on tawa, cook until golden on both sides.

Vegetable Sandwich

  1. Spread butter and chutney on bread.
  2. Add sliced veggies.
  3. Grill until golden.

Dhokla

  1. Mix batter, add Eno, and steam for 15 minutes.
  2. Temper with mustard seeds and curry leaves.

Thepla

  1. Knead flour with spices and fenugreek.
  2. Roll and cook with oil until golden.

Chole Bhature

  1. Make dough, roll, and fry bhature.
  2. Cook chickpeas with spices.

Bread Upma

  1. Sauté mustard seeds and onions.
  2. Add tomatoes and torn bread.
  3. Cook until combined.

Oats Porridge

  1. Boil oats with milk until thick.
  2. Sweeten with honey and serve warm.

Medu Vada

  1. Grind soaked dal, add spices.
  2. Shape into donuts and fry until golden.

Puri Bhaji

  1. Make a soft dough with flour, roll into small circles, and deep fry until golden.
  2. Sauté mustard seeds, green chilies, and turmeric, then add boiled potatoes and salt.
  3. Mix well, cook for a few minutes, and serve hot with puris.
