Butter Chicken

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices.
  2. Cook in butter, add tomato puree and cream.
  3. Simmer and serve with naan or rice.

Biryani

  1. Marinate meat with spices and yogurt.
  2. Layer cooked rice with marinated meat.
  3. Cook on low heat until flavors meld.

Tandoori Chicken

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and tandoori spices.
  2. Grill or bake until cooked through.
  3. Serve with onion rings and lemon.

Fish Curry

  1. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices in oil.
  2. Add fish and simmer in coconut milk.
  3. Serve with rice or bread.

Chicken Vindaloo

  1. Marinate chicken in vinegar and spices.
  2. Cook with onions and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Prawn Masala

  1. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices in oil.
  2. Add prawns and cook until done.
  3. Serve with rice or naan.

Mutton Rogan Josh

  1. Sauté onions and spices in oil.
  2. Add mutton and cook until tender.
  3. Serve with rice or naan.

Egg Curry

  1. Boil and peel eggs.
  2. Cook onions, tomatoes, and spices.
  3. Add eggs and simmer. Serve with rice.

Chicken Korma

  1. Cook onions and spices in ghee.
  2. Add marinated chicken and yogurt.
  3. Simmer until cooked. Serve with naan.

Lamb Biryani

  1. Marinate lamb with spices and yogurt.
  2. Layer with partially cooked rice.
  3. Cook on low heat to blend flavors.

Fish Tikka

  1. Marinate fish in yogurt and spices.
  2. Skewer and grill until cooked.
  3. Serve with mint chutney.

Kadai Chicken

  1. Cook onions and spices in a kadai.
  2. Add chicken and cook until tender.
  3. Garnish with coriander and serve.

Chettinad Chicken

  1. Grind spices and sauté with onions.
  2. Add chicken and cook until tender.
  3. Serve with rice or parotta.

Mutton Seekh Kebab

  1. Mix minced mutton with spices and herbs.
  2. Shape onto skewers and grill until cooked.
  3. Serve with green chutney.

Chicken Chettinad

  1. Blend spices into a paste.
  2. Sauté onions, tomatoes, and the spice paste.
  3. Add chicken, cook until tender. Serve with rice.
